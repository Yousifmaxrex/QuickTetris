Tetris Facts!

  1. Tetris was created in 1984 by Alexey Pajitnov, a Russian software engineer.
  2. The game was inspired by Pentominoes, a mathematical puzzle where you arrange shapes made of five squares.
  3. The name "Tetris" combines "tetra," referring to the four squares in each block, and "tennis," Pajitnov's favorite sport.
  4. The iconic Tetris theme music, "Korobeiniki," is a Russian folk song.
  5. The current world record holder for Tetris is Alex Thach. He achieved an incredible score of 16,700,760 points in 2024. Thats a lot of numbers!
